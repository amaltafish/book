ORT BRAUDE LIBRARY


Welcome!

The central library serves all academic units within the college.
The library's collection comprises over 230,000 items,
including textbooks, literature, periodicals, projects, CDs, transparencies, lectures, regulations, prints, and maps.
Most of the items in the collection are digital, including over 148,000 e-books,
around 31,000 digital STM (Science, Technology, and Medicine) periodicals, 267 recorded lectures,
and a collection of projects totaling 3,192, including 70 technician degree projects, 1,830 engineering degree projects,
1,215 bachelor's degree projects (BTECH + BSC), and 75 master's degree projects.
The lending collection is constantly replenished,
providing students with a semestral and annual borrowing option for textbooks and texts at low cost,
managed by the campus's administration.
The library houses 34 computer stations, with 26 available for student use,
along with photocopying machines, printers, and scanners.
